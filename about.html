About Travelo+

Travelo+ offers innovative software solutions for hotels, resorts, vacation rentals, and restaurants, serving over 1,000 properties across 50+ countries. Our comprehensive cloud-based ERP system optimizes daily operations and back-office functions while automating marketing activities—helping hospitality businesses achieve maximum efficiency and growth. Travelo+ provides end-to-end solutions that cover every department within the hospitality sector, available either as standalone modules or as an integrated All-in-One System.
Founded in 2019 and headquartered in Bangalore, India, Travelo+ is a Private Limited Company established by Siddharth Goenka. Coming from a family hotel business, Siddharth identified the industry’s inefficiencies and technological gaps. Leveraging his technical expertise, he recognized the growing need for automation and advanced software in hospitality, inspiring him to create solutions not only for his own hotels but for the entire industry.

Core values of Travelo+

Innovation

We believe to come up with new innovative solutions, we have to think out of the box and challenge all existing assumptions about the industry and market. True innovation can only be achieved if we have problem solving & ideation as a core value in every aspect of thinking & design.

Customer Centricity

We keep our customers & users (hospitality providers) at the centre of all our decision-making. All aspects of product design, performance, features are done by empathising with our customers and ensuring they are prioritized.

Simplicity

We are deeply inspired by the idea of keeping things simple. We believe that most hospitality technology products were made unnecessarily complicated, and the same results can be achieved with simple design & thinking to solve for customer pain point and promote ease of use.

Authenticity

All decisions at Aiosell are taken by being truly authentic to ourselves, our team, our customers, our partners, our competitors, and to the industry at large, because we believe our ethics should never be compromised for any short term win.

Travelo+ – Vision & Mission

Our Vision: To revolutionize the hospitality industry by creating advanced technology solutions that empower independent hospitality providers to reach their full potential, achieving maximum results with minimal effort.

Our Mission: To become the world’s leading full-stack hospitality system that unifies every aspect of the business—from marketing to operations—into one simple, affordable, and user-friendly platform, enabling hospitality owners to simplify management and maximize profitability.
